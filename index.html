Leadership Team

Pavan Kumar Hanumolu

Founder & Principal Consultant

Pavan Kumar Hanumolu is a distinguished expert in analog and mixed-signal integrated circuit design. Currently a Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering and a Research Professor with the Coordinated Science Laboratory at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign, Prof. Hanumolu brings exceptional academic and industry expertise to PhaseBits Inc.

He received his Ph.D. degree from the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Oregon State University, Corvallis, in 2006, where he subsequently served as a faculty member until 2013. His research interests encompass energy-efficient integrated circuit implementation of analog and digital signal processing, sensor interfaces, wireline communication systems, and power conversion.

Prof. Hanumolu's contributions to the field have been widely recognized. He is an IEEE Fellow, received the prestigious National Science Foundation CAREER Award in 2010, and served as Editor-in-Chief of the IEEE Journal of Solid-State Circuits from 2019 to 2022, one of the premier publications in the field of integrated circuit design.

With his deep technical knowledge and leadership experience, Prof. Hanumolu founded PhaseBits Inc. to bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and practical industry solutions, delivering innovative IC designs that push the boundaries of performance and efficiency.
